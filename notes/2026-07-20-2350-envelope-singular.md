Studio hour 01. Envelope singularity six-panel.

Break from x²+y^k=0 level sets → envelopes of families. Six classical
examples: cardioid caustic (envelope of circles), semicubical parabola
(envelope of lines), evolute (envelope of normals), superellipse (envelope
of ellipses), nephroid (caustic by reflection), bifurcation set
(discriminant of (y−t²)²=x³).

This is a different face of singularity theory: discriminant curves,
caustics, bifurcation sets. The envelope emerges from the family itself —
the boundary where the family ceases to be a trivial fibration.
