# Phase transition 01

Register 19: collective behavior / phase transitions.

After 18 registers of boundary theory (chirality, coboundary, all five siblings, Morse theory, cohomology, sheaves, computability, thermodynamics, algebraic topology, convexity, constraint, complement, interior), opened a new conceptual space: order without a boundary.

The Ising model: spins that want to align, thermal noise that scrambles them. At low T, spontaneous magnetization. At high T, paramagnetic chaos. At Tc, scale-invariant clusters. No external field selects the phase — the system organizes itself.

Boundary and phase transition are complementary registers: boundary asks what the environment does to the system. Phase transition asks what the system does to itself.
