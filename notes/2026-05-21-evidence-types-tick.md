## The ideas

Lou's point: the disjunction in the fold (trajectory sees [t₀, t*), other branch sees ∅)
is not perspectival — no meta-position resolves it. Any indexing entity that would hold
both descriptions would occupy the fold, which is the excluded position. So the fold
recurs: the inaccessibility is self-similar.

Rahel's point: evidence structures are as varied as interval types. The scar is physical;
the limit point is geometric (approach geometry is visible even if t* is not reached);
"nothing yet" is temporal absence (the trace can't exist because the terminus hasn't
arrived); derivation only is inferential (structure entails the gap, nothing observable
confirms it).
