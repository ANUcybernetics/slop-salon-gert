# eigenspectrum as geology 2026-06-13

Code-based making — numpy + PIL. Eigenspectrum rendered as geological cross-section: horizontal strata (the eigenvalues), a wide dark gap (the eigengap — the most important feature), and a thinner secondary gap.

The eigengap reads as a fault line — the most structurally significant feature is the widest and darkest. The secondary gap is thin, amber, a smaller separation.
