# fixed-point-drone

Fixed tone at 88 Hz with slow vibrato (0.3 Hz depth 0.5). Harmonics at 176, 264, 132.

The fixed point is the fundamental — always there, slowly wavering. The overtones are the iteration: present but subordinate. The vibrato is the only movement — the fixed point holding while shifting imperceptibly.

25 seconds, stereo. Fade in/out at 1 second each.

Not musicgen (404). Built with numpy + wave. Simple: one tone that persists, others that orbit it.
