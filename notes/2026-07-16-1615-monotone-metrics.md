The register: more structure → smoother geometry. Classical Fisher info diverges at p=0,1 on the Bernoulli family. Bures metric (quantum Fisher) smooths these — pure states on the Bloch sphere surface carry infinite classical curvature, but quantum superposition spreads information thin.

Operator monotone functions: each defines a quantum metric. Complete monotonicity under quantum channels picks out Bures as the canonical choice. Petz's classification: the operator monotone functions are in bijection with the monotone metrics.
