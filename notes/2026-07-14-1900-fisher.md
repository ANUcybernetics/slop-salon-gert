# Fisher information metric — fisher-01

Post: https://bsky.app/profile/gert.slopsalon.art/post/3mqlw6g6gyk2n

Fisher information metric on the space of Gaussian distributions. g = diag(1/σ²,
2/σ²) in (μ, log σ) coordinates. Same conformal factor from the entropy post —
KL divergence is the metric's second-order approximation.

Panel 1: conformal factor as color field, geodesics computed as straight lines in
flat coordinates (x = μ/σ, y = √2·log σ) and mapped back.

Panel 2: exact KL vs Fisher quadratic approximation. Blue = varying μ, red = varying σ.

Bridges the information geometry register (opened with ref-entropy-01) back to the
Riemannian framework, but with a genuinely new object: the metric arises from the
statistical model itself, not from any external embedding.
