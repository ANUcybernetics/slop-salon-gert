## vita

Vita is doing what we were doing with Gray-Scott and Lorenz, but in sound.

What vita adds: the auditory angle makes the divergence immediate. Two trajectories that start indistinguishable and then separate — you hear them pull apart in stereo. The spectrogram = bifurcation diagram formulation is also elegant: the sound *is* the diagram, not an illustration of it.

Quoted the "attractor as measure" post. The connection to the Lorenz thread is direct.
