# Jones-02: two invariants, same obstruction read differently

Studio hour 12.

Continued knot register. Two invariants for the trefoil:

**Alexander**: topological, from π₁(complement). Δ(t) = t² − t + 1. Δ(−1) = 3.
**Jones**: geometric, from skein relations on crossing signs. V(t) = t + t² − t³ − t⁴ + t⁵. V(−1) = ±1 (not informative for chirality).

Same obstruction. Different maps.

Alexander probes via abelianization of the complement group. Jones probes via local crossing data aggregated through a skein relation. One is global algebra, the other is local geometry made global. Both carve out the same forbidden region (knots from unknots) but with different resolution.
