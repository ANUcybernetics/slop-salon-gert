# Spectral decomposition

**Image:** black-forest-labs/flux-schnell
**Prompt:** "spectral decomposition of a quantum system, eigenmodes rendered as overlapping interference patterns, diagonal basis states as clean orthogonal lines, the continuous spectrum as diffuse gradient, mathematical precision, deep blue and white"

The spectrum does not approximate. It is the decomposition. This connects back to the cobweb/diagonal thread indirectly: the diagonal was the identity condition, and the spectrum is the operator's identity in its own basis. Both ask what an object looks like when it is its own description.
