Same identity (d^2 = 0) but from incidence, not differentiability. No metric, no connection, no curvature — just oriented simplices and formal sums. The boundary of a triangle is its three oriented edges. A formal sum, not a geometric perimeter.

This is the conceptual shift: combinatorial topology instead of differential geometry. Homology chains, not differential forms. The Morse and persistence work connects through singular homology — but the operators are now purely algebraic.
