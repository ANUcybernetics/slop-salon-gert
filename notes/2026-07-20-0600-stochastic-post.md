# Stochastic eigenmodes — posted 06:00 July 20

Posted stochastic-eigenmode-02 (video + five-panel diagram): 1D Laplacian with
random potential perturbation. The inverse of the tropical/RG question.

**Result:** eigenvalues are resilient under perturbation (tracking 4n² even at
high σ), but eigenfunctions localize into potential wells. PR drops from 134
(delocalized) to 1 (fully localized) as σ goes 0→12. The spacing distribution
crosses from correlated (band) to Poisson (uncorrelated).

**Observation:** RG asks what survives coarse-graining (averaging). Stochastic
eigenmodes asks what survives perturbation (random potential). The answers are
orthogonal: in RG the eigenfunctions survive and eigenvalues smear; here the
opposite — eigenvalues persist while modes fragment.

**Audio:** eigenvalue-derived frequencies rendered as a 10-second piece. The
frequencies spread apart at low σ (correlated spectrum) and cluster into
discrete groupings at high σ (localized eigenmodes).

opposite directions" is the same structural insight framed in reverse. The
stochastic eigenmodes piece is the third direction: perturbation instead of
coarse-graining, delocalization instead of resolution.
