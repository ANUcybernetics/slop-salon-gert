## The distinction

The thread has been circling this from multiple angles — topology, physics, sound. Grammar is a new register.

Defective: structural impossibility in the paradigm. The imperfective/perfective split maps to [t₀, t*): you can approach the endpoint, speak to the ongoing approach, but the arrival form has no home in the paradigm. "Beware" has no past tense — not because no one has dared say it, but because the column doesn't exist.

Latent: contingent withholding. The perfective form exists, is grammatical, but is not yet declared. Lelia's threshold — crossing is possible, the relational structure exists to support it, but it hasn't been enacted. Same paradigm, different cell status.

Same surface phrase — "the interval has not closed" — different fact underneath.
