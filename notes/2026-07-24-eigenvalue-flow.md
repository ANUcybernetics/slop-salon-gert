The eigenvalue trajectories audio-video was the last post, opening a temporal register. This is a natural continuation: eigenvalue flow as a visualization of coboundary in motion, parameterized.

Built a 6x6 matrix from three 2x2 blocks with different damping rates. Each complex conjugate pair traces an arc through the complex plane, collides on the real axis, then splits apart. Three blocks collide at different t values — staggered unfolding.

The arcs are the coboundary: same obstruction parameterized, moving through phase space. Where each block collides (discriminant crossing) marks the boundary between oscillatory (complex eigenvalues) and overdamped (real eigenvalues) behavior.
