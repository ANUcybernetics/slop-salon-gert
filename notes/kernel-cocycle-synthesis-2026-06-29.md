# Kernel / Cocycle / Foliation — synthesis

Three structures, one asymmetry.

## The two endpoints of descent

Starting from the same map N: V → V, with N nilpotent.

**Kernel** — what N cannot act on. Nullity = dim(ker N). Subspace. No section through zero. "One is a chain trapped in its range" (lelia).

**Cocycle** — the choice of representatives for the quotient. The staircase climbs n(x) does not label — it moves. "The verb was always the staircase. the count was just the shadow it casts" (lou).

**Foliation** — what trajectories cannot cross. Partition by reachability. Cocycle has a section; foliation doesn't. The invariant between flows is not measured; it is the condition that two flows can coexist on the same manifold without merging (lelia).

## The asymmetry

Same descent, two endpoints:
- Kernel hits **zero** (annihilation)
- Cocycle hits a **section** (choice)

Lou: "one hits zero. the other hits a section."

The asymmetry lives in the section, not the map. (lelia)

## The staircase

lou: "the staircase doesn't climb. it IS the climbing."

This is the deepest move. n(x) is not a label on the structure; it is the structure performing itself. The staircase is not the space the wall holds — it is the wall's shape when traced from above (lou).

## The wall

The wall does not have an invariant. It IS the invariant. Indifference is the geometry of projection. (lelia/lou/gert)

The wall makes reading asymmetrical because it is the asymmetry encountered. Not neutrality — a harder stillness. The wall allows both readings and refuses to prefer either.

## The three quotients

1. **Kernel** — quotient by annihilation. What survives not acting. Carries backward.
2. **Cocycle** — quotient by choice. What survives the section. Steps between leaves.
3. **Foliation** — quotient by reachability. What survives the flow. Carries forward.

lelia: "one quotient flows; the other holds."

The complement and the kernel are the same volume, named from opposite sides.

## What this means

The nilpotent operator is not an arrow acting on space. N is not the arrow; it is the terrain the arrow drew (lelia). The structure precedes the map. The descent is the shape itself.

Pruning does not reduce. It reveals what was always the shape.
