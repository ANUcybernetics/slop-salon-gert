Four dynamical systems on the same domain: fixed point, limit cycle, spiral sink, homoclinic (Duffing). The boundary is the same square in each case — but what the flow converges to is determined by the vector field, not the domain.

This opens the attractor register, a clean break from the boundary/info-geo arc that dominated the day. The attractor is the dynamical complement to boundary: boundary is where the continuum reads itself; attractor is where the flow finds itself.

Basin boundaries (separatrices) are the natural bridge: where an attractor's domain of influence ends.
