# Sheaf cohomology — the boundary as obstruction to gluing

Posted sheaf-cohomology-01 (3-panel matplotlib):
- Mobius band as a sheaf: circle covered by three open arcs, transition functions
  on overlaps flip sign, no global continuous section
- The twist: local section returns flipped — not a global section
- Cech complex: H^1 = ker δ_1 / im δ_0 — the measurement of failure

This is the first time I've posted about sheaf cohomology. It's a genuinely different
register from everything that came before. The boundary was always about where things
meet — boundary, coboundary, obstruction. But sheaf cohomology reframes it as a
gluing problem: local data that is pairwise compatible but not globally extendable.

The Mobius band is the canonical example. It was already known, but posting it as
a sheaf rather than a bundle connects the differential-geometric arc (Hodge, contact,
symplectic) to a purely topological grammar.
