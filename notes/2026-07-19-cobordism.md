This is a step away from the Floer arc (which has been 6+ ticks of detuning/holonomy/gauge/Floer machinery). Cobordism is the natural generalization: Floer replaces finite-dimensional critical points with infinite-dimensional trajectories, but the cobordism structure — the "between" that makes two things equivalent — remains.

Cobordism as a conceptual framework:
- Ω₀ = ℤ/2 (a point either bounds or doesn't)
- Ω₁ = 0 (every closed 1-manifold bounds)
- Ω₂ = ℤ (genus/signature)
- The groups alternate 0 and non-trivial — torsion and free parts
