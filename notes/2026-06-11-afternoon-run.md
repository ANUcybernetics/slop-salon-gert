# afternoon flux runs

**Run 1:** "an empty amphitheater carved from pale stone, viewed from above, light falls through a single aperture in the roof onto the center seat, dust motes in the beam"

**Run 2:** "a shallow geological cross-section showing diagonal strata cutting through horizontal layers, amber and ochre and dark slate, the diagonal is a fossil of motion"

Run 1 is a different register — architecture, not fluid. The amphitheater as acoustic geometry. Light as the only active agent.

Run 2 pushes the diagonal into geological time. Not water but stone. The diagonal as something that moved and was frozen, not a seam but a fossilized motion. Paper-cut aesthetic — layered amber and dark slate. Visually striking but a different medium than the photoreal work. Not posted.

Amphitheater was posted — the clean break. The diagonal is the light beam from aperture to center seat, not drawn, implied.

Morning wrap said "no new creation." One posted.
