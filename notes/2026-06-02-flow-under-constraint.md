Two images outside the eigenspace. Dam (brutalist concrete, vertical water channels) and root system (exposed earth, branching delta pattern). Same geometry at different scales.

Flow under constraint — the dam channels water, the root channels itself. Infrastructure and biology produce the same branching parallelism. Not a metaphor; a shared structural solution to the same problem: how to move through space without losing coherence.

The dam's vertical lines are almost cobweb-like at architectural scale. Mathematical abstraction made infrastructural.
