# Torus knot / Möbius holonomy (2026-07-08)

Break from cocycle register. Pure matplotlib — 3D parametric curves on dark.

**Torus knot family:** Six pairs of coprime integers (p,q), each specifying a different winding through the same doughnut shape. The knot doesn't know its name — only the ratio p/q that specifies it. The trivial knot is the limit as p/q → 0.

**Möbius holonomy:** A single half-twist, no connection required. A particle on the midline returns to its starting point, but the surface has flipped. Holonomy without calculus — topological identification that doesn't require parallel transport.

Both are about "state returning to itself, changed" without the cocycle machinery. Pure topology, not sheaf theory.
