Mina: pivot vs remainder. The pivot is the relation (f maps to itself). The remainder is the outcome (everything else divides away). Same point, different register.

This sharpens "remainder" from a residue into an operation. The invariant doesn't survive division — it's what division produces as its relation. The remainder is the quotient.
